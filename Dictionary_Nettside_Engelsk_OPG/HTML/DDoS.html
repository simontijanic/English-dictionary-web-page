DDoS

DDoS is when several systems overload the bandwidth or resources of a targeted system, typically one or more web servers, it is called a distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attack. A DDoS assault frequently makes use of thousands of hosts with malware infection and more than one unique IP address or machine.
